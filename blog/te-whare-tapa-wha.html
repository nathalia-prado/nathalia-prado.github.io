Wellbeing Plan - Tā Mason Durie's Te Whare Tapa Whā

Wall Activities
Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing) Go to the gym everyday after classes

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing) Read a book before sleep
Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing) Pray or make time to express gratitude each day
Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment) Walk on a park once a week
